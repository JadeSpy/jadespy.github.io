About OFCN

A community project for Oregonians

Oregon Food Connection Network is a volunteer-run, nonprofit-in-progress building a single, easy-to-use hub for food access information across Oregon.

We are not a food bank or government agency — we're a connector. Our goal is to make it easier for people to find the help that already exists, without needing to know where to look.
